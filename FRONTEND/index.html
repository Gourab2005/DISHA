Electricity

Some of the major problems that we face in cities regarding electricity are no street lights, frequent power cuts, poor maintenance of electric wires, and many more. We collaborate with various companies and mainly startups to tackle these problems.

Water

Water is one of the most important thing to sustain life. Although, a lot of water is available throughout the globe but all of it not usable. We bring forward complaints raised by people about blocked drainage systems and broken public taps, in the views of authority and provide solutions for them.

Roadways

The cheapest and most used means of transport is by Roadways. But, the condition of roadways is not really good nowadays. In bigger cities and towns it maybe all fine but the rural areas are still neglected. We may become the voice for them and put forward this issue before the administration and also provide solutions to the problems.
